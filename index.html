I am a sustainability and ESG compliance professional with a legal background (LL.M.) and an MBA in Sustainable Business Development, specialising in supply chain due diligence and environmental and social compliance.

My work focuses on sustainable agriculture and global supply chains, where I analyse ESG risks, regulatory requirements, and stakeholder dynamics across international contexts. I have experience supporting ESG governance, compliance frameworks, and sustainability initiatives, including leading a multi-country B Corp certification process and contributing to deforestation-free supply chain approaches (EUDR context).

I combine legal expertise and systems thinking to understand complex value chains and support the implementation of EU sustainability regulations such as EUDR and CSRD. I am particularly interested in how organisations can translate regulatory requirements into practical, data-informed, and measurable actions.

Project

Tools & Methods

Systems Mapping | ESG Risk Analysis | Regulatory Analysis

This project analyses sustainability challenges and compliance gaps in global coffee supply chains using a systems thinking approach, with a focus on EU regulatory requirements such as the EUDR.

The analysis identifies key leverage points for improving environmental and social outcomes, including strengthening stakeholder participation, scaling regenerative agricultural practices, improving waste management, and enhancing gender inclusion.

The project highlights the importance of traceability systems, regulatory alignment across jurisdictions, and the integration of ESG risk analysis into supply chain management to support both compliance and long-term sustainability.
