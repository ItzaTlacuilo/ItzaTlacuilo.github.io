Project

Mapping the Coffee Supply Chain

Tools & Methods
Systems Mapping | ESG Risk Analysis | Regulatory Analysis

This project analyses sustainability challenges and compliance gaps in global coffee supply chains using a systems thinking approach, with a focus on EU regulatory requirements such as the EUDR.

The analysis identifies key leverage points for improving environmental and social outcomes, including strengthening stakeholder participation, scaling regenerative agricultural practices, improving waste management, and enhancing gender inclusion.

The project highlights the importance of traceability systems, regulatory alignment across jurisdictions, and the integration of ESG risk analysis into supply chain management to support both compliance and long-term sustainability.
